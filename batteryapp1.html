 Home of the Atlanta Braves, retail stores, restaurants, businesses, and apartments. The Battery Atlanta aka Suntrust Park opened in 2017. Located in Smyrna, Georgia 10 miles northwest of downtown Atlanta.

Duration Oct 2018 - Nov 2018

Team Sharif York, Attiyya Akinwole, Eugene Seoh, Ena Kadric

Objective

Improve the population of the Battery. We wanted to bring in that exposure to enhance businesses around the Battery during the off season.

Overview

Problem

 

The Battery and its retail locations have a significant drop-off in traffic and business after the baseball season ends.

 

Solution

 

Our solution is to design an app that helps generate business to the Battery in the offseason.  We aim to do this by creating features that consolidate all retail, dining, and event details into one uniform mobile platform.  Our goal is to increase visibility, awareness, and exposure for the Battery, as well as making a visit to the Battery a more streamlined, efficient, and an overall more enjoyable experience.

 

My Role

 

As a team we used various research methods to understand the problem and synthesized our findings. As a team member I focused on creating personas of people who represented our target users. After that I created a customer journey map using on of the personas. I then sketched out wireframes and designed the onboarding process of the app interface. Then after the initial design phase I created an app flow to understand the users flow throughout the app.
